Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 11, 2026

1. What This Policy Covers

This policy explains what information Villages Golf Cart Trader ("we," "us") collects when you use villagesgolfcarttrader.com, how we use it, and the choices you have. We keep this simple: we collect what we need to run a local classified listing site, and we do not sell your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account information: your email address and login credentials when you create an account.
  • Listing content: the details you choose to publish — cart description, photos, asking price, general location (community name), and the contact details you include for buyers.
  • Messages: anything you send us through the contact form.
  • Payment information: payments are processed by Stripe. Your card number goes directly to Stripe and never touches our servers; we receive only confirmation of payment and basic transaction details.
  • Technical data: standard server logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited) used for security and to keep the Site working, and cookies/local storage needed for login sessions.

3. Listings Are Public — and Shared

Listings exist to be seen. Anything you include in a listing (photos, description, price, location, and the contact information you choose to display) is publicly visible on the Site and may appear in search engines. Active listings are also automatically shared to our social media channels, including our Facebook page, to give your cart more local exposure. Don't include anything in a listing that you don't want public — for example, you may prefer to share your street address with serious buyers privately rather than in the listing itself.

4. How We Use Information

  • To operate the Site: publish your listings, manage your account, and process payments.
  • To communicate with you about your account, listings, or support requests.
  • To promote listings on the Site and our social channels, as described above.
  • To prevent fraud, enforce our Terms of Service, and meet legal obligations.

5. Who We Share Information With

We share information only with the service providers that make the Site run:

  • Stripe — payment processing (see Stripe's own privacy policy for how it handles payment data);
  • Supabase — our database, where account and listing data is stored;
  • DigitalOcean — hosting infrastructure for the Site;
  • Meta (Facebook) — when listings are auto-shared to our Facebook page, the public listing content appears on Meta's platform;
  • authorities, if required by law or to protect the safety of our users.

We do not sell or rent your personal information to anyone.

6. Retention & Deletion

Expired listings may remain in our records to support renewals and prevent fraud, but are no longer publicly displayed. You can request deletion of your account and associated personal information at any time by contacting us; we will delete it except where we must retain records (for example, payment records required for tax and accounting purposes). Note that copies of listings already shared to social media may need to be removed there separately, and we will do so on request for posts on our own page.

7. Security & Children

We use reputable providers, encrypted connections (HTTPS), and access controls to protect your information, though no online service can guarantee perfect security. The Site is intended for adults; it is not directed to anyone under 18, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Your Choices & Contact

You can edit your listings from your account, choose what contact details appear publicly, and request a copy or deletion of your personal information. Florida residents and visitors from regions with privacy laws granting additional rights (such as access, correction, and deletion) may exercise those rights by contacting us.

Questions or requests: use the contact page or email info@villagesgolfcarttrader.com. We may update this policy from time to time; the effective date above reflects the latest version.
