Listing Renewal Rules

How listing expiration and renewal works for private sellers and dealers.

👤 Private Sellers

Private seller ads are paid 30-day listings. They do not auto-renew, and you will never be charged without your action.

  • ● Email reminder 7 days before expiration
  • ● Email reminder 3 days before expiration
  • ● Email reminder on the expiration day
  • ● Seller may renew, edit, mark sold, or let expire
  • ● Expired listings are hidden from public search but retained in your account for easy renewal

🏪 Dealers

Dealer packages are monthly subscriptions that renew automatically until cancelled.

  • ● Dealer inventory remains active while subscription is active
  • ● Dealers can edit, add, remove, or mark listings as sold anytime
  • ● Cancel anytime — no long-term commitment
  • ● Renewal receipt emailed each billing cycle

💬 Questions about renewals?

Contact us through the contact page and we'll help you out. We never charge without your action for private seller listings.
